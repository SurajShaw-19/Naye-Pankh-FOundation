Our Story

NayePankh Foundation

NayePankh Foundation is a UP Government-registered non-governmental organisation driven by a strong desire to serve society and make it a better place for all. Founded with a spirit of youth-powered change, we have grown into one of India's biggest student organisations.

From distributing free food and sanitary pads to running education drives and clothing campaigns, our volunteers bring hope to thousands every month. Our work is completely tax-exempt under 80G of the Indian Income Tax Act.

Our Journey

Milestones

2017

Foundation Established

NayePankh Foundation was born in Kanpur with a small group of passionate students determined to serve their community.

2019

UP Government Registration

Officially registered as an NGO by the Uttar Pradesh Government, gaining credibility and legal standing.

2021

80G & 12A Certification

Achieved tax-exempt status under the Indian Income Tax Act, enabling donors to claim exemptions on contributions.

2023

2 Lakh+ Beneficiaries

Crossed the milestone of helping over 200k+ underprivileged individuals with food, education, sanitary products, and clothing.

Now

Growing Every Day

Active in 50+ cities, 5,000+ volunteers, and expanding our reach to touch more lives across India.
