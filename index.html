What We Stand For

Mission & Vision

🎯

Our Mission

To serve as the bridge between resources and those who need them most — providing free food, education, clothing, and healthcare to underprivileged communities across India through the power of student volunteerism.

🌅

Our Vision

A society where every individual, regardless of economic status, has access to dignity, education, and opportunity. We envision a future where no child goes hungry and every young mind gets the chance to soar — NayePankh (New Wings).

💛

Our Values

Compassion, transparency, and community-first action. We believe "Service to mankind is the service to God" and that collective action — however small — can solve the largest problems.

"If we all do something, then together there is no problem that we cannot solve!"
— Prashant Shukla, Founder & President, NayePankh Foundation
